FAQ

What is skate.? ▼ ▲
skate. is the newest entry in the EA Skate franchise. It's a free-to-play skateboarding game that offers a realistic experience with authentic physics and controls. Players can perform tricks, explore open-world environments, and interact with a skateboarding community. It's a highly anticipated release for fans of the franchise and newcomers alike.
What is the graphic creator? ▼ ▲
The Graphic Creator is a tool used to design custom graphics for in-game skateboards and clothing. It allows players to create their unique visual styles by providing various options and features to customize their skateboarding gear. With the Graphic Creator, players can unleash their creativity and personalize their in-game experience by designing and applying their own graphics to their skateboard decks and clothing items. It offers a way for players to express their individuality and make their virtual gear stand out in the game.
How do I upload my graphic? ▼ ▲
Users can upload their custom designs by pressing the "Upload Design" button. This action generates a unique code that, when entered in the game, retrieves the graphic, enabling players to apply it to their in-game gear. It offers a seamless way to personalize and enhance the visual elements of the game to reflect individual creativity and style.
Can I play skate. on multiple platforms? ▼ ▲
skate. is available on popular platforms such as PlayStation, Xbox, and the EA Launcher on PC. This means that players can enjoy the game on their preferred gaming consoles or PC systems. Additionally, exciting news for players looking to connect with friends across different platforms is that skate. supports cross-play functionality. This enables players on PlayStation, Xbox, and PC to skate together and enjoy the game's multiplayer features seamlessly, regardless of their platform of choice.
Does skate. offer cross platform saves? ▼ ▲
Yes, skate. offers cross-platform saves. The game utilizes your EA account to link and store your save data. This means that your progress and save data will be associated with your EA account, allowing you to seamlessly transfer and access your game progress across different platforms. Whether you switch from PlayStation to Xbox or PC, your save data will remain linked to your EA account and be available for you to continue your skateboarding journey.
